Banking and Finance Services:

**Our expertise includes advice in relation to:**

**Banking Regulations:**

We provide comprehensive legal guidance on banking regulations, ensuring compliance with statutory requirements, regulatory guidelines, and industry standards to support sound banking practices.

**Securities Related Matters:**

Our legal team offers expert advice on securities laws and regulations, including securities offerings, compliance requirements, disclosure obligations, and regulatory filings.

**Structuring and Documentation:**

We assist clients in structuring complex financial transactions and preparing tailored documentation, including loan agreements, security documents, and other financial instruments, to meet their specific financing needs.

**Structured Escrow Transactions:**

We advise on structured escrow transactions, facilitating secure and efficient handling of funds and assets pending fulfillment of specified conditions or completion of transactions.

**Bankruptcy and Insolvency Proceedings:**

We represent clients in bankruptcy and insolvency proceedings, offering strategic guidance and advocacy to creditors, debtors, and other stakeholders in distressed financial situations.

**Disputes Arising in Financing and Securitization Arrangements:**

We handle disputes arising from financing and securitization arrangements, representing clients in negotiation, mediation, arbitration, or litigation to resolve conflicts and protect their interests.

**Debt Restructuring:**

We provide legal assistance in debt restructuring processes, including debt renegotiation, rescheduling, and restructuring agreements, to facilitate sustainable debt management and recovery.

**Advising on the Regulatory Framework and Related Matters:**

We offer advisory services on the regulatory framework governing banking and finance, including compliance requirements, regulatory changes, and risk management strategies.

**Fundraising from Domestic and International Markets:**

We advise clients on fundraising initiatives, including equity offerings, debt issuances, and other capital raising activities, in both domestic and international markets.

**Due Diligence:**

We conduct thorough due diligence reviews to assess legal and regulatory risks associated with financial transactions, providing clients with comprehensive insights to make informed decisions.

**Drafting and Negotiation of Term Sheets and Transactional Documents:**

We draft and negotiate term sheets, loan agreements, security documents, and other transactional documents, ensuring clarity, enforceability, and alignment with clients' objectives.

**Recovery and Enforcement Proceedings:**

We assist clients in recovery and enforcement proceedings, including debt recovery actions, foreclosure proceedings, and enforcement of security interests, to maximize recovery of outstanding debts and assets.
