OUR INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY & CYBER PRACTICE COVERS

Software Protection & Licensing Approvals:

We provide comprehensive legal assistance in software protection, including obtaining licensing approvals and drafting documentation to safeguard our clients' intellectual property rights in software assets.

Data Protection of Brands and Concepts:

We offer strategic advice and legal solutions for data protection of brands and concepts, ensuring compliance with data privacy laws and regulations to safeguard sensitive information and intellectual property.

Resolution of Disputes Associated with Name:

Our legal team assists clients in resolving disputes related to trademarks, domain names, and brand identity, including domain name disputes, trademark infringement claims, and brand protection strategies.

Technology Transfer and Technical Assistance Agreements:

We draft and negotiate technology transfer and technical assistance agreements, facilitating the exchange of technological know-how, intellectual property rights, and technical expertise between parties.

Online Unfair Use or Infringement of Material Possession Rights:

We provide legal representation and advocacy in cases of online unfair use or infringement of material possession rights, including copyright infringement, trademark violations, and unauthorized use of digital content.

Investigation of Cyber Crimes:

Our team conducts thorough investigations into cyber crimes, including hacking, phishing, cyber stalking, cyber squatting, and credit card frauds, leveraging forensic expertise and legal strategies to gather evidence and pursue legal remedies.

Formation of Contracts and Legal Vetting of Web Pages:

We assist clients in the formation of contracts related to information technology and cyber matters, as well as provide legal vetting of web pages to ensure compliance with applicable laws and regulations.

Online Arbitration Associated with Cyber Law Issues:

We offer online arbitration services for resolving disputes arising from cyber law issues, providing efficient and cost-effective alternative dispute resolution mechanisms tailored to the digital environment.

Resolving Jurisdictional Issues:

We advise clients on resolving jurisdictional issues in cyber law matters, including determining the appropriate forum for dispute resolution and navigating conflicts of laws in cross-border transactions.

Proceedings Before Adjudicating Officer:

We represent clients in proceedings before adjudicating officers appointed under cyber laws, ensuring effective legal representation and protection of clients' interests in regulatory proceedings.

Representing Clients in Tribunals and Courts:

We represent clients in Cyber Regulatory Tribunal, Cyber Regulatory Appellate Tribunal, High Courts, and the Supreme Court in matters regarding internet fraud, hacking, privacy, and security-related issues, advocating for our clients' rights and interests at every stage of the legal process.
